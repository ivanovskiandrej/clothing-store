Frequently Asked Questions

What is your return policy?

Return any unused or defective merchandise at our store at the American University of Bulgaria campus or by mail. Returns made within 30 days of the order shipment date will be refunded to the original form of payment. Returns made after 30 days of the order shipment date will be refunded via an emailed e-merchandise credit.


Can I change my order?

We start working on your order as soon as we get it, so we are unable to make any changes or modifications to an order after it has been placed and confirmed.


Why is my order shipping in multiple packages?

To get your items to you ASAP, we sometimes need to ship different items from different locations like our multiple distribution centers and retail stores.


What payment methods do you accept?

Skaptopara Clothing Store accepts the following forms of payment: Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Paypal and Skaptopara Clothing Store Gift Cards.


Do you offer price adjustments?

We're happy to issue a one-time price adjustment for an item from our website if it goes on sale within 14 days of your order shipment date. A refund for the price difference will be issued back to the original form of payment. If you used a Gift Card to make your purchase, a new gift card will be issued reflecting the difference. UrbanOutfitters.com is unable to grant price adjustments for purchases made in our retail stores.
